<p><p>The Pneumatic Steam Valves market includes two main types: Single Acting Steam Valves and Double Acting Steam Valves. Single Acting Steam Valves operate using pneumatic pressure to control the steam flow in one direction, making them simpler and cost-effective for applications requiring less control precision. In contrast, Double Acting Steam Valves utilize pneumatic pressure for both opening and closing mechanisms, offering greater control and responsiveness. This makes them suitable for applications requiring high accuracy and reliability in steam management.</p></p>
