<p><p>The bottled water market consists of two primary types: natural mineral water and man-made mineral water. Natural mineral water is sourced from underground springs, containing minerals that are naturally present, and is often marketed for its health benefits. In contrast, man-made mineral water is produced by adding minerals to purified water, allowing for controlled mineral content and taste customization. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers but differ in their sourcing and production methods, influencing market preferences.</p></p>
