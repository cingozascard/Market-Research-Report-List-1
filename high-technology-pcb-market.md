<p><p>The high technology PCB market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Multi-Layer Rigid PCB**: Consists of multiple layers for complex circuitry, often used in advanced electronics.  </p><p>2. **HDI (High-Density Interconnector) PCB**: Features finer lines and smaller vias for compact designs, ideal for mobile devices.  </p><p>3. **Rigid-Flex PCB**: Combines flexible and rigid circuits, suitable for space-constrained applications.  </p><p>4. **Heavy Copper PCB**: Utilizes thicker copper layers for power handling, used in high-power applications.  </p><p>5. **High Frequency PCB**: Designed for radio frequency and microwave applications, ensuring signal integrity.  </p><p>6. **Others**: This category includes specialized types catering to niche markets or unique requirements.</p></p>
