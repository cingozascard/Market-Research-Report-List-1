<p><p>The Extrusion Coating Grade LDPE market features two primary production methods: Tubular Process and Autoclave Process. The Tubular Process involves melting LDPE resin and extruding it through a circular die, resulting in a continuous film that can be used for various packaging applications. In contrast, the Autoclave Process utilizes high-pressure conditions to polymerize LDPE, yielding a more uniform and thicker coating suitable for specific industrial uses. Both methods cater to different product requirements and market demands in packaging and coating applications.</p></p>
