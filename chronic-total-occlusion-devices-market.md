<p><p>The Chronic Total Occlusion (CTO) devices market includes several key categories. Micro catheters are specialized, thin tubes designed for precise navigation within blood vessels. Guide wires assist in maneuvering these catheters through occluded arteries. Crossing devices are engineered to penetrate and traverse blockages effectively, while re-entry devices help clinicians navigate back into the true lumen after crossing a lesion. Together, these devices enhance the effectiveness of interventional procedures for treating chronic total occlusions, improving patient outcomes in cardiovascular care.</p></p>
