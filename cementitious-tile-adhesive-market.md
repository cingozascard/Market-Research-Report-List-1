<p><p>The Cementitious Tile Adhesive market is categorized mainly into two types: Ordinary Tile Adhesive and Enhanced Tile Adhesive. Ordinary Tile Adhesive is a basic formulation used for standard installations, providing adequate adhesion for regular tiles in typical environments. In contrast, Enhanced Tile Adhesive offers improved performance features, such as higher bond strength, flexibility, and water resistance, making it suitable for demanding applications, including wet areas or large format tiles. These distinctions cater to varying customer needs in tile installation.</p></p>
