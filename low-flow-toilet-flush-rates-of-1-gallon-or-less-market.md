<p><p>Low-flow toilets are designed to conserve water, using flush rates of 1 gallon or less. The market is primarily divided into two types: single-flush and dual-flush toilets. Single-flush models provide a consistent flush volume for all waste, making them simple and straightforward. Dual-flush toilets offer two flush options—one for liquid waste and another for solid waste—allowing users to select the appropriate flush, further enhancing water conservation. Both types contribute to sustainable practices in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
