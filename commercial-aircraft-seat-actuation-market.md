<p><p>The commercial aircraft seat actuation market consists of two primary types: hydraulic and electromechanical seat actuation systems. Hydraulic seat actuation systems use fluid pressure to move and position seats, offering strong performance and reliability, especially in larger aircraft. Electromechanical seat actuation systems leverage electric motors and gear systems to achieve seat adjustments, providing precise control and flexibility in design. Both types cater to the growing demand for comfort and functionality in passenger seating solutions across various aircraft platforms.</p></p>
