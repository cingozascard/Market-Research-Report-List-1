<p><p>The mulch colorant market primarily features two types: carbon-based dyes and iron oxide-based dyes. Carbon-based dyes are derived from organic materials and offer vibrant colors while being environmentally friendly. They are often favored for their natural appearance. In contrast, iron oxide-based dyes are mineral-based and provide durability and resistance to fading, making them suitable for long-lasting color in landscaping applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences for aesthetics and functionality in mulch products.</p></p>
