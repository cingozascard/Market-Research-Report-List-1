<p><strong>The Drive Shaft (Driveshaft) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Half Shaft</li><li>Propeller Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The driveshaft market comprises two main types: half shafts and propeller shafts. Half shafts are typically used in front-wheel and all-wheel-drive vehicles, transmitting torque from the differential to the wheels. They are crucial for ensuring power delivery and vehicle stability. Propeller shafts, on the other hand, are found in rear-wheel drive and four-wheel drive vehicles, connecting the transmission to the rear axle. Both types are essential for transferring power efficiently and supporting vehicle movement across various terrains.</p></p>
