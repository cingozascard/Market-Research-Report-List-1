<p><p>The automotive embedded systems market encompasses two primary segments: embedded hardware and embedded software. Embedded hardware includes components like microcontrollers, sensors, and communication interfaces that enable vehicle functionality and connectivity. Conversely, embedded software refers to the programming and algorithms that integrate these hardware components, managing tasks such as vehicle control, diagnostics, and infotainment systems. Both segments are crucial for enhancing vehicle performance, safety, and user experience, driving innovation in modern automotive technologies and supporting the development of autonomous vehicles.</p></p>
