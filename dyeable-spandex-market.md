<p><p>The dyeable spandex market primarily includes two types: acid dyes and others. Acid dyes are water-soluble dyes that are ideal for dyeing nylon and wool fibers, leveraging their affinity for the amino groups in these materials. They provide vibrant colors and excellent fastness. The "others" category encompasses various dye types, such as disperse dyes for polyester blends and reactive dyes for cotton, catering to different fiber compositions. This diversity allows for a wide range of applications in textiles and fashion.</p></p>
