<p><p>Equipment maintenance software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based maintenance software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the system from anywhere with internet connectivity. This type often offers greater scalability, automatic updates, and reduced infrastructure costs. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on company servers, providing greater control and customization while requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different business needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
