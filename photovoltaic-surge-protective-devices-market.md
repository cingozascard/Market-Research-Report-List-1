<p><p>The Photovoltaic Surge Protective Devices (SPD) market features three primary types. Type 1 devices provide direct strike protection, safeguarding systems from lightning impacts. Type 2 devices focus on limiting overvoltages, ensuring that transient surges do not damage the photovoltaic system. Type 1+2 devices combine both functionalities, offering complete protection against direct strikes and overvoltages. This comprehensive approach ensures the longevity and efficiency of solar installations by minimizing the risk of electrical damage from multiple surge events.</p></p>
