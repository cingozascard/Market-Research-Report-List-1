<p><p>The rubber additive market is primarily divided into three types: antidegradants, accelerators, and other additives. Antidegradants protect rubber from degradation due to heat, oxygen, and ozone, enhancing its durability. Accelerators speed up the vulcanization process, improving production efficiency and product performance. Other additives include fillers, processing aids, and colorants, which enhance the physical properties and processing characteristics of rubber products. Together, these additives play a crucial role in optimizing rubber formulations for varied applications.</p></p>
