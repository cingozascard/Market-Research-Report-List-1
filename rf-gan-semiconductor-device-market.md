<p><p>The RF GaN semiconductor device market comprises three main types: GaN-On-SiC, GaN-On-Silicon, and GaN-On-Diamond. GaN-On-SiC offers superior thermal conductivity and power handling, making it ideal for high-performance applications like radar and satellite communications. GaN-On-Silicon provides a cost-effective solution with good efficiency for consumer electronics, albeit with lower performance compared to SiC. GaN-On-Diamond technologies leverage excellent thermal management for high-frequency operations, although they are still emerging and typically more expensive, targeting specialized applications.</p></p>
