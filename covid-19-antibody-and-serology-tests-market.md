<p><p>The COVID-19 antibody and serology tests market includes various testing methods. High Throughput Chemiluminescent Microparticle Immunoassay (CMIA) offers automated, rapid results for large sample volumes. Lateral Flow tests provide quick, easy-to-use results, ideal for point-of-care settings. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) is a sensitive lab-based method for detecting antibodies. Enzyme-Linked Fluorescent Assay (ELFA) combines aspects of ELISA with fluorescence detection for enhanced sensitivity. Other methods include various novel approaches and alternative technologies catering to different diagnostic needs.</p></p>
