<p><p>The Crucible Induction Furnace market is segmented into two primary types: Medium Frequency Induction Furnaces and High Frequency Induction Furnaces. Medium Frequency Induction Furnaces operate typically in the range of 1 kHz to 10 kHz, making them suitable for melting ferrous and non-ferrous metals efficiently. In contrast, High Frequency Induction Furnaces, exceeding 10 kHz, are ideal for precise applications like heat treatment and small-scale melting, providing enhanced control and faster heating rates. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
