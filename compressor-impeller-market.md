<p><ul><li>Open Style</li><li>Semi-open Style</li><li>Closed Style</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compressor impeller market is categorized into three primary types: open style, semi-open style, and closed style. Open style impellers feature blades directly attached to the hub, allowing for easy handling of large volumes of gas but with lower efficiency. Semi-open impellers offer a balance between performance and versatility, suitable for various applications. Closed style impellers, designed with enclosed blades, provide high efficiency and better pressure delivery, making them ideal for applications requiring precise airflow control.</p></p>
