<p><p>The Enterovirus and Parechovirus Testing Market includes two primary testing methods: RT-PCR and Rapid Tests. RT-PCR (Reverse Transcriptase Polymerase Chain Reaction) is a molecular technique that detects viral RNA, offering high sensitivity and specificity for diagnosing infections. Rapid Tests provide quick results, usually within hours, using immunological methods to identify viral antigens or antibodies. While RT-PCR is preferred for its accuracy in clinical settings, Rapid Tests are favored for their convenience and speed, making them suitable for point-of-care testing.</p></p>
