<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Glucose Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glucose Oxidase Method</li><li>Glucose Dehydrogenase Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glucose meter market is primarily segmented into two types based on the testing methods: Glucose Oxidase and Glucose Dehydrogenase. The Glucose Oxidase method uses an enzyme that facilitates the oxidation of glucose, producing a measurable signal, often used in traditional blood glucose meters. In contrast, the Glucose Dehydrogenase method employs a different enzyme that directly measures glucose levels, offering advantages like reduced interference from other sugars and improved accuracy. Both methods cater to diverse user needs and preferences.</p></p>
