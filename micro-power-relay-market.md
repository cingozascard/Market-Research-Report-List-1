<p><p>The Micro Power Relay Market is categorized into two main types: Closed Type and Open Type. Closed Type relays provide enhanced protection against environmental factors, making them suitable for applications in harsh conditions. They typically feature sealed housing that prevents dust and moisture ingress. In contrast, Open Type relays are more versatile and economical, allowing for easy access during installation and maintenance. They are commonly used in applications where protection from the environment is less critical.</p></p>
