<p><strong>The Rotating Torque Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact-based sensing</li><li>Noncontact-based sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotating torque sensors market is broadly divided into two types: contact-based and noncontact-based sensing. Contact-based sensors measure torque through direct mechanical engagement, often resulting in higher accuracy but potential wear over time. In contrast, noncontact-based sensors utilize electromagnetic or optical technologies to gauge torque without physical contact, offering benefits like reduced maintenance and improved durability. Both types serve various applications, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial machinery, catering to different operational requirements and performance standards.</p></p>
