<p><strong>The Medical Fixation Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Fixation Devices</li><li>External Fixed Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Fixation Devices market is categorized into two main types: internal fixation devices and external fixation devices. Internal fixation devices, such as plates, screws, and intramedullary nails, are used to stabilize fractured bones internally, promoting healing with minimal disruption to surrounding tissues. External fixation devices, on the other hand, are employed outside the body, utilizing pins and frames to immobilize and support fractured limbs or joints, often used in complex fractures or when internal fixation is not feasible.</p></p>
