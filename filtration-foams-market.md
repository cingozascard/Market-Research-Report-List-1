<p><ul><li>Open Cell Foam</li><li>Closed Cell Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Filtration foams are categorized mainly into open cell foam and closed cell foam. Open cell foams feature interconnected pores, allowing air and fluids to flow freely, making them ideal for applications requiring sound absorption and lightweight filtration. Conversely, closed cell foams have sealed cells that resist moisture penetration and provide superior insulation, making them suitable for demanding filtration applications in industrial and automotive uses. Both types serve distinct purposes based on their structural characteristics and filtration needs.</p></p>
