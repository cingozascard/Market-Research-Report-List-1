<p><p>The digital production printer market comprises primarily two types: Inkjet and Electrophotography printers. Inkjet digital production printers use liquid ink sprayed onto substrates, offering high-quality images and versatility for various materials. They excel in short runs and customized prints. Electrophotography digital production printers, on the other hand, utilize toner and a process similar to photocopying, providing consistent quality and speed, ideal for high-volume jobs. Both types are essential for meeting the diverse demands of modern print production.</p></p>
