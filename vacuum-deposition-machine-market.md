<p><strong>The Vacuum Deposition Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD)</li><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum deposition machine market primarily comprises two key technologies: Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) and Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD). PVD involves the physical transfer of material from a source to a substrate, often used for coatings with superior hardness and optical properties. In contrast, CVD relies on chemical reactions to produce vaporized precursors, which deposit onto substrates to create thin films with excellent uniformity and adhesion. Both methods are widely utilized in industries like electronics, automotive, and optics.</p></p>
