<p><p>The kitchen food garbage processors market is categorized into two main types: connection type and independent type. Connection type processors are integrated with the plumbing system, allowing waste disposal directly into the sewage, leveraging existing water flow. Independent type processors, on the other hand, operate without reliance on plumbing, handling food waste internally, often featuring standalone designs. Both types cater to varying consumer needs, with connection types favored for their convenience and independent types appreciated for flexibility and ease of installation.</p></p>
