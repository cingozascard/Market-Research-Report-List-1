<p><p>Resort management software is categorized into two primary markets: on-premises and SaaS-based. On-premises solutions require installation on local servers within a resort, offering greater control and security but necessitating more upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, SaaS-based software is cloud-hosted, allowing for easy access, scalability, and lower upfront costs. This model typically operates on a subscription basis, making it appealing for resorts seeking flexibility and reduced IT management burdens while ensuring updates and support are seamlessly integrated.</p></p>
