<p><p>The aluminum extrusion die market is primarily divided into two types: hollow molds and solid molds. Hollow molds are designed to create complex shapes with internal voids, enabling the production of lightweight and structurally sound components used in applications like pipes and frames. On the other hand, solid molds are used for manufacturing solid profiles, providing strength and durability for structural elements. Both types play a crucial role in various industries, including construction, automotive, and aerospace, catering to specific design and performance requirements.</p></p>
