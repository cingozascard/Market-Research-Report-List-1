<p><p>Remote Data Concentrators (RDC) are devices used in various industries to gather, process, and transmit data from remote locations to centralized systems. The market for RDC can be divided into two main types: Standard Type and Wireless Type. Standard Type RDCs typically use wired connections to communicate, offering reliable data transmission in stable environments. In contrast, Wireless Type RDCs leverage wireless communication technologies, providing flexibility and mobility, making them ideal for challenging environments where wiring is impractical or costly.</p></p>
