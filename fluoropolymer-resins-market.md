<p><strong>The Fluoropolymer Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Fluoropolymer resins encompass various types, each with unique properties and applications. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is renowned for its high-temperature resistance and low friction, making it ideal for non-stick coatings and gaskets. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) offers excellent chemical resistance and is often used in piping and coatings. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) combines the benefits of PTFE and FEP, providing flexibility and chemical resistance. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) is valued for its transparency and ease of processing. Other types include ETFE and THV, serving specific niche applications.</p></p>
