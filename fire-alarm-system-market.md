<p><strong>The Fire Alarm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Fire Alarm Systems</li><li>Addressable Fire Alarm Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire alarm system market is primarily categorized into two types: Conventional and Addressable Fire Alarm Systems. Conventional systems use a series of zones to detect fire, alerting occupants but lacking precise location identification. They are cost-effective for smaller buildings. In contrast, Addressable systems provide real-time information, identifying the exact location of smoke or heat detection, allowing for quicker response and easier troubleshooting. They are more suitable for larger or complex facilities, enhancing overall safety and efficiency.</p></p>
