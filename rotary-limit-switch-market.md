<p><strong>The Rotary Limit Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gear Type</li><li>Encoded Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary limit switch market comprises two main types: gear type and encoded type. Gear type rotary limit switches utilize mechanical gears to determine the position of a rotating shaft, providing reliable on/off control in various applications. Encoded type rotary limit switches, on the other hand, use electronic encoding techniques to provide precise rotational data, enabling more complex control and feedback mechanisms. Both types play crucial roles in automation, robotics, and industrial applications where precise position sensing is essential.</p></p>
