<p><p>The jet hand dryer market can be categorized into two main types: hand-in and hand-under models. Hand-in dryers require users to insert their hands into a designated cavity, where high-speed jets of air quickly dry the hands. In contrast, hand-under dryers operate by allowing users to position their hands beneath a nozzle, receiving airflow from above. Both designs focus on efficiency and hygiene, offering environmentally friendly alternatives to paper towels in restrooms by reducing waste and promoting rapid drying.</p></p>
