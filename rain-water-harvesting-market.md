<p><ul><li>Above Ground</li><li>Underground</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rainwater harvesting involves two primary market types: above ground and underground systems. Above-ground systems typically include rain barrels and surface storage tanks that collect and store rainwater for immediate use, such as irrigation and household needs. In contrast, underground systems consist of subterranean tanks or cisterns that capture rainwater, providing space-efficient storage and protection from evaporation and contamination. Both types cater to varying space, budget, and efficiency requirements, making them suitable for diverse residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
