<p><p>The LED encapsulant market consists primarily of two key types: epoxy and silicone. Epoxy encapsulants are widely used for their excellent adhesion, durability, and chemical resistance, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Both types play a crucial role in protecting LED components from moisture and mechanical damage, ultimately enhancing longevity and performance.</p></p>
